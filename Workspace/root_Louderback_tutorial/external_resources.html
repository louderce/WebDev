Here are a few great links to other blogs containing great recipies and other resources!

This site is still brand new and a work in-progress. I wanted to share some of my favorite tried-and-true resources with you. The links below are reources that I myself have used in the past, or currently use.

Oh She Glows - Vegan food blog

Fun fact: I was vegan for two years for health reasons. I learned SO much from this blog; many of the recipes I still use today. I love that this blog focuses on hearty, veggie-forward meals that fill you up and give you plenty energy! No imitation meat substitute here, just great tasting, satisfying food so good, you won't miss the meat, I promise!

Shameless Plug! My personal food blog - This Savory Life

This is a food blog that I started a few years ago at the request of my friends and family. It is built with wordpress and does not meet the requirements for this assignment, but it cotains some of my favorite creations. I really should get around to adding more content!

Fantastic recipe for curried lentel soup - Masoor Dal Tadka

One of my best friends referred this recipe to me, and its one of my favorie go-tos. Make sure to read the introduction to the recipe; there is really great information on how to get the best flavor outcome, including methods and explainations!
